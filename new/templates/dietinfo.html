Obesity

calorie-deficit diet : Choose nutrient-dense, whole food

Cholesterol

Mediterranean Diet : Emphasizes fruits, vegetables, whole grains, fish, and healthy fats like olive oil. Portfolio Diet: Includes cholesterol-lowering foods like plant sterols, nuts, oats, and soy.

Heart Health

DASH Diet (Dietary Approaches to Stop Hypertension): Emphasizes fruits, vegetables, whole grains, lean proteins, and low-fat dairy. Mediterranean Diet: Promotes heart-healthy fats, fish, fruits, and vegetables.

Acidity

Low-Acid Diet: Focuses on non-citrus fruits, vegetables, and lean proteins. GERD Diet (Gastroesophageal Reflux Disease): Avoids acidic, spicy, and fatty foods.

Blood Pressure

DASH Diet: Emphasizes potassium-rich foods, whole grains, lean proteins, and limits sodium. Mediterranean Diet: Incorporates heart-healthy fats, fruits, vegetables, and fish.

Diabetes

Balanced Diet: Includes a variety of nutrient-dense foods from all food groups. Mediterranean Diet: Offers a balanced and heart-healthy approach to eating.
